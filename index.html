About Us

Empowering Growth
Across Southern Africa

Founded in 2022 in Gaborone, Botswana, we have extensive experience helping businesses identify and prioritize the best opportunities for growth. We approach our clients' challenges with passion and energy, conducting exhaustive research and tailored data analysis.

In 2024 we expanded into neighbouring South Africa, establishing our presence in Johannesburg — cementing our position as a leading player in the regional tech and business consultancy industry.
